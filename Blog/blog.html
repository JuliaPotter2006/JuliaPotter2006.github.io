My Mission

My name is Julia, and reading is one of my greatest passions. I've loved to read ever since I was three years old; literature is a staple of my life. However, I've recently become disillusioned with the state of modern fiction. In my opinion, there are so many novels being published nowadays that one must dig through piles of mediocrity to find worthwhile literature. Personally, I have not found a genuinely amazing, recently published piece of fiction in several years. At this point, I'm no longer interested in trying.

With no stock in the present and little hope for the future, there's only one place to go- the past. So, I've decided to embark on a journey to read as many classic literature novels as possible and document my progress by writing reviews for each book I read.

It is my mission to become a cultured reader. Won't you come along for the ride?
