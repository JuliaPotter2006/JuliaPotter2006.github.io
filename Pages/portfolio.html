  • Academic Projects
    • Data Analysis Report - Intro to Data Science
      • In this report, I explored research questions regarding the popularity of songs and how their traits affect how they ranked on the Spotify Top Songs chart. Does a song's BPM (beats per minute) influence its rank on the Spotify charts? Do songs in the major mode rank higher than songs in the minor mode? Can a song's rank on the Spotify charts, and by extension its popularity, be predicted by its characteristics? The answers I found in this report brought about valuable insight regarding the reasons why certain songs become more popular than others.
